About Us

Built by Collectors, Run Like a Business.

Atlantic TCG is a New York-based online trading card game retailer — built by people who actually play and collect the games we sell.

Who We Are

Atlantic TCG is an online trading card game retailer based in New York, operating out of Long Island. We're collectors and players first — the kind of people who track set releases, sleeve our pulls, and care about the long-term health of the hobby. That perspective shapes how we run the business: we sell the way we'd want to be sold to.

What We Carry

We specialize in Bandai trading card games, including the Gundam Card Game, One Piece Card Game, Dragon Ball Super Card Game, and Digimon Card Game. We also carry Pokémon products and a growing selection of accessories — sleeves, deck boxes, and the supplies players and collectors rely on to protect what they love.

How We Operate

Trust is everything in this hobby, so we keep our standards simple and consistent:

  • Authentic products. We source genuine, verified product — never reprints, counterfeits, or grey-market substitutes.
  • Careful packaging. Singles are protected appropriately, and sealed product ships with protective packaging so it arrives the way it left us.
  • Fast shipping. Orders are processed promptly, with tracking included on every shipment.
  • Honest customer service. Clear answers, fair policies, and real communication — no games off the table.

Where We're Headed

Our long-term goal is to become a trusted destination for collectors and players across the games we support. As we grow, we plan to expand beyond online retail into local events, conventions, and community engagement — supporting the players and collectors who make this hobby what it is.
